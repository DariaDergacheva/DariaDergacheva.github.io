About

I am an applied researcher and consultant at the Platform Governance, Media and Technology Lab (ZeMKI), University of Bremen. My work sits at the intersection of platform governance, AI and content moderation, and digital regulation. I also bring expert-level knowledge of Russian and post-Soviet information environments, including state-aligned disinformation and platform manipulation.

I am currently seeking remote research and consultancy roles (EU & international), including contract-based engagements.

DSA transparency Researcher data access AI moderation GenAI governance Disinformation (Russia) Mixed methods

Core expertise

  • Platform governance & regulation: transparency, accountability, systemic risks, and researcher access to data (DSA context).
  • AI-enabled content moderation: automated moderation, overblocking, and governance of AI/GenAI in platform decision-making.
  • Disinformation & influence operations: Russia-focused expertise; how state-aligned campaigns exploit platform policies and algorithms.
  • Applied research delivery: policy briefs, consultation reports, presentations, and executive-ready synthesis.
  • Methods: computational social science (APIs, large-scale social data) + qualitative research (in-depth interviews, coding tools).

Consulting services

Typical engagement formats (remote): short advisories, research sprints, and ongoing retained support.

  • Regulatory & policy analysis: DSA transparency, data access, and platform accountability.
  • Risk & systemic impact assessments: content moderation, overblocking, and AI governance risks.
  • Disinformation research briefs: Russia and post-Soviet influence ecosystems; platform exploitation patterns.
  • Research design & methods: mixed-methods research design, API-based data collection, and analysis plans.
  • Stakeholder presentations: concise executive decks and workshops for non-academic audiences.
  • Evidence synthesis: translating academic literature and primary data into decision-ready recommendations.

Selected applied work

  • Platform Governance Archive (Project lead): day-to-day curation of a dataset of platform policies.
  • DSA transparency database research: analysis of platform content decisions using the DSA Transparency Database (24h dataset study).
  • Horizon 2020 — ReCreating Europe: mixed methods research using YouTube API and interviews with EU-based visual artists; contributed to policy recommendations.
  • AI discourse longitudinal study: ten years of Twitter/X data in multiple languages; stakeholder mapping in AI discourse (US and Germany).

Teaching & leadership

  • Lecturer (University of Bremen): AI and the media; platform governance; computational methods in communication science.
  • Co-curator of PhD program at ZeMKI (one of two curators).
  • Regional editor and researcher (Global Voices), leading teams of journalists and translators on tech and policy coverage.
